❓ How to Play
Movement
Use the D-Pad arrow buttons to move Aneeq across the isometric world. The ↻ Turn button rotates your facing direction clockwise. Press OK to interact with nearby tiles.
Biomes
Plains – Flat grassland, safe to explore. Forest – Dense trees with fruit. Mountains – Ore patches and cave entrances. Water – Impassable but scenic. Beach – Farmable sandy tiles. Snow Peak – Icy summits.
Survival
Health hearts drain when in dangerous biomes. Hunger bar depletes over time – eat Wheat or Carrots to restore it. Full hunger restores health. Exp bar fills from harvesting and crafting.
Crafting
Open Inventory 💼, switch to 🔨 Craft tab. Collect Iron Ore, Wood, and Apples to unlock recipes like the Iron Pickaxe and Wooden Hoe.
Commands
Tap 💬 to open Chat. Type /give @s [item] [qty] to add items. Example: /give @s Apple 10
